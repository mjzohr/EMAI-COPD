Omics Datasets
==============

This section covers molecular modalities used in EMAI-COPD Tier 1, including:

- Proteomics
- Metabolomics
- Transcriptomics

All datasets are derived from the COPDGene Phase 2 (P2) cohort and undergo modality-specific preprocessing pipelines to ensure consistency and quality for downstream modeling.
